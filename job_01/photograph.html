The Outbreak

March 25,1971,Pakistani military launched Operation Searchlight,starting brutal campaign mass atrocities.Sheikh Mujibur Rahman declared independence in the early hours of March 26.

Liberation War

The Bangladesh Liberation War of 1971 was an armed conflict that resulted in the independence of Bangladesh from Pakistan. Spanning nine months, the war began on March 26, 1971.

Victory Day in Bangladesh

On this day, the Pakistani Eastern Command officially surrendered at the Ramna Race Course in Dhaka, marking the end of the nine-month Liberation War and the genocide.

High-resolution Photographs of 1971 Liberation War

The Bangladesh Liberation War, which occurred in 1971, was a nine-month armed conflict between Bengali nationalists in East Pakistan (now Bangladesh) and the Pakistani military junta based in West Pakistan.
