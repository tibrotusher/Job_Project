Dish Description

A standard single-patty meat burger averages 418 calories and provides a balanced profile of protein and carbohydrates. It is a classic fast-food and comfort dish found worldwide, consisting of a grilled ground meat patty tucked inside a sliced bun, usually accompanied by various fresh toppings and savory condiments.The traditional burger features a seasoned ground meat patty cooked via grilling, pan-searing, or smashing. It is placed inside a soft round bun (such as sesame or brioche). Typical fresh layers include lettuce, sliced tomatoes, onions, and pickles. Popular condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise add moisture and tanginess.
